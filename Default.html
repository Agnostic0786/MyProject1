WHAT WE DO

India's Blood Centers' member organizations make up India's largest network of non-profit community blood centers, which operate more than 600 blood donation collection sites. Together the network provides half the India blood supply to over 3,500 hospitals and healthcare facilities. India's Blood Centers is not affiliated with the American Red Cross. How Can be My Blood Type Determined

It’s inherited. Like eye color, blood type is passed genetically from your parents. Whether your blood group is type A, B, AB or O is based on the blood types of your mother and father.
